Pepsi announced that it will cooperate with some of the most influential women in the NFT field, and will release a new set of selected NFT series focusing on female artists at the first "Women's Power Festival and Summit" held on May 6-7. These women include Rachel Winter, an artist of Remarkable Women NFT series, Shaylin Wallace, a digital and graphic designer who focuses on surreal art, Amber Vittoria, an artist of It's because You're Pretty NFT series, and Varvara Alay, an artist of Flower Girls NFT series.
It is reported that this NFT series will be issued by POAP (Proof of Attendance Protocol), that is, only those who attend the Women's Strength Festival and Summit can get the unique NFT collection of this series.
